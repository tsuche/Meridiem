Corona Simulation

Welcome to our site! We hope everyone is doing well during these tough times of sheltering in place. Remember to drink plenty of water, get enough sleep, and wash your hands! This project's inspiration came from the current pandemic. It's a game built primarily focusing on mental health and its influences. Further information of the game can be access below the website and the video. Stay safe and have fun!! (we challenge you if you are able to find all the easter eggs) :)




to play and download game, go to the github link https://github.com/Girl2Wolf/SummerQuarantine.git
